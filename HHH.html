About Sarah

Hon. Sarah Namugwanya is a passionate advocate for the people of Kampala Central. With over 15 years of experience in community service and local government, Sarah has dedicated her life to improving the lives of Ugandans.

Born and raised in Kampala, Sarah understands the challenges faced by her constituents and is committed to bringing positive change to the community. Her background in law and public administration equips her with the knowledge and skills necessary to effectively represent the people in Parliament.
